About the Event

Join us for the SUPER LEAGUE FOOTBALL 2024, a spectacular football tournament showcasing the best university teams. The event will take place on August 25th, 2024, at the University Stadium. Don’t miss out on exciting matches, vibrant fan zones, and special attractions that make this event a must-see!

Participating Teams

Thunder Strikers

The Thunder Strikers are known for their aggressive gameplay and swift attacks, making them one of the most feared teams in the league.

Blaze Warriors

The Blaze Warriors bring a perfect mix of defense and offense, with a history of turning the tide in their favor during crucial moments.

Storm Breakers

The Storm Breakers are the underdogs, known for their resilience and ability to surprise even the strongest opponents.
